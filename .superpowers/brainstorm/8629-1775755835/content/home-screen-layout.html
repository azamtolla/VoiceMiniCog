Design Notes

  • Cards: White on #F1F5F9 background, 16px radius, HA-style shadow (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.12))
  • Icons: SF Symbols in SwiftUI (bolt.fill, person.2.fill, list.clipboard.fill) — emojis shown here for mockup
  • Animation: Staggered fade-up on appear (0.1s delay between cards). Press: scale 0.97 spring.
  • Touch targets: Entire card is tappable, well above 60pt minimum
  • Typography: Title 20pt semibold, description 14pt regular, duration 13pt semibold — all SF Pro
  • Accent colors: Quick=#007AFF (blue), Caregiver=#5E5CE6 (indigo), Extended=#30D158 (green)
  • Resume banner: Only visible when AssessmentPersistence has an interrupted session
