Animation Specs (SwiftUI)

Entrance Header fades in first (0.1s delay). Cards stagger in with fade + 20pt upward slide, 0.45s easeOut, 0.12s delay between each.
Press Scale 1.0 → 0.96 → 1.0 over 0.3s using Material 3 standard easing. Light haptic (UIImpactFeedbackGenerator .light).
Shadow lift On press, shadow stays constant (no elevation change) — keeps it calm.
SwiftUI impl .opacity(appeared ? 1 : 0) + .offset(y: appeared ? 0 : 20) with .animation(.easeOut(duration: 0.45).delay(index * 0.12)). Press via .scaleEffect(isPressed ? 0.96 : 1.0) with spring(0.15s, 0.85 damping).
